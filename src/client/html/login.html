Welcome to the
ATLAS Spotlight Generator!

At the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, the ATLAS Internship Program facilitates a variety of pre-professional experiences for a diverse population of driven and dedicated interns. With the variety of intriguing and current projects and persons involved in the program, ATLAS Intern Spotlights were created to highlight and celebrate the achievements of our students. Each and every ATLAS Intern leaves their internship with a number of stories to tell, and Intern Spotlights are where those stories live.

If you are a part of the ATLAS Intern Team, you can use this website as a way to help brainstorm, draft and manage your Spotlight Paragraphs!
